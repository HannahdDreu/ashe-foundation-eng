Naserian's Story

Update Project Enkishon

Fantastic news!

Naserian's shop is up and running, and she has been managing it for almost 2 months now.

Thanks to your donations, we have reached our goal! Namely, that Naserian has been able to open her own shop. This milestone fundamentally changes her life and that of her children. She now has a stable source of income, which allows her to support her family and send her children to school. In addition, the shop is an important resource for the local Maasai community, where residents can buy clothes without having to travel long distances.

This success would not have been possible without the support of all the involved donors. Your contribution has made a lasting impact on Naserian and her family’s lives, and it gives her the opportunity to build her independence and future. Once again, a heartfelt thank you for your generosity and involvement in this wonderful project!



What’s next?

We will continue to provide updates about the clothing shop on this page. Naserian will also continue to receive support from the Ashe Foundation to keep her business successful. The guidance mainly consists of advice on sourcing the clothes and developing a long-term vision for the shop.
