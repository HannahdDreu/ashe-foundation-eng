Become a Donor and Make a Lasting Impact for the Maasai in Tanzania

At Ashe Foundation, we work daily to strengthen the socio-economic position of the Maasai community in Tanzania. As a small-scale foundation, we have direct contact with the people we help. We work closely with the Maasai and understand their needs, because the projects and campaigns we undertake are initiated from within the Maasai community.

This allows us to confidently say that every donation is used directly and effectively to improve lives and make dreams come true. To make projects like Neema's—who, thanks to owning her own shop, now has a stable income to support her family—possible, we need your support.

By becoming a regular donor, you help us achieve sustainable change. Your donation provides not only economic opportunities but also hope and self-sufficiency to a community that is often overlooked.

As a regular donor, you can be assured that your contribution directly reaches the Maasai. Together, we can create a future where families can earn their own livelihood, and children have access to basic services and education. With your help, we will create lasting, positive change.

Join Ashe Foundation and help build a stronger, more equal Tanzania—for the Maasai, and for the generations to come.
