Our mission


Ashe Foundation is working towards a world where the Maasai community in Tanzania has opportunities to pursue a good life and their own happiness.
Regardless of cultural background, gender, lifestyle, or belief. Everyone should have the opportunity to thrive. That's what Ashe Foundation stands for.

In addition, livelihood security is guaranteed. Also, the assurance of basic needs provision, such as access to food, clean drinking water, medical facilities, education, good housing, and participation within their own community, are included in this.

Ashe Foundation collaborates with the local Maasai community to create socio-economic opportunities. Through local partnerships, Ashe Foundation supports sustainable local projects. The intended impact of Ashe Foundation is a world where the Maasai community has opportunities to pursue a good life and their own happiness.

Our strategies

And how do we achieve this kind of world?


Strategy 1.

Ashe Foundation supports local projects in a sustainable manner, which are embraced by the local Maasai community. By providing support, opportunities can be created to strengthen the socio-economic position of the Maasai community.

This strategy is directly aimed at improving the living conditions of the Maasai community. Supporting local projects by Ashe Foundation occurs within four domains:

  • Income
  • Agriculture
  • Health and Well-being
  • Education

    • Strategy 2.

    Ashe Foundation closely collaborates with local partners from the Maasai community. The effect of our close collaboration is that the projects are embraced by the Maasai community.

    This strategy aims to create support and therefore sustainability. The support from the Maasai community contributes to the sustainability of the projects.
